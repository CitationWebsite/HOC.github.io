Hi, welcome to the House of Citation.

Houseofcitation.com is an easy to use and free website which automates referencing by generating citations for you.

It is the only citation generator on Google which accurately automates the ILI Citation Style, formulated by the Indian Law Institute.

Contact us at: support@houseofcitation.com.

How does houseofcitation.com benefit you?

It can be used by law students, professors, practitioners, to prepare the footnotes for Research Papers, College Assignments, Moot Courts, and wherever else the citation format is to be followed. It is your personal citation machine!

This citation engine has been created to provide a free online tool to make citation management easier. It is not affiliated to any institution which has formulated the different citation styles.
